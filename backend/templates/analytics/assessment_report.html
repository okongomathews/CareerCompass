Average Compatibility Scores

Understanding the three scores:

Personality (P): Average MBTI→career alignment. High = students' personality types strongly match recommended careers.

Academic (A): Subject→career alignment. High = students' subjects align with recommended careers (e.g. Sciences → Medicine).

Market (M): Average Kenyan labour-market demand of recommended careers. High = careers are in-demand in Kenya.

Overall: Weighted composite of all three. Primary quality indicator.

If bars show identical low values (e.g. 5% everywhere), this means only one student has data — averages equal that student's individual scores. More student data produces meaningful distributions.
