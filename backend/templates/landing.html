How it works

Four steps. Nothing hidden.

Every number on this page is the same one you'll see inside the product — we're not going to show you a nicer version on the way in.

1

Take the assessment

60 questions, 15 per dimension, about 15–20 minutes. Answers autosave, so closing your browser mid-way is fine — it picks up exactly where you left off.

2

Get your matches

A weighted score blends your personality type, academic background, and learning style into a ranked list of careers — explained below, not hidden behind the score.

3

Talk to your AI coach

Ask follow-up questions about subjects, universities, or specific careers, day or night — it understands the Kenyan education system, not just generic career advice.

4

Build your path

Each career comes with the subjects it needs and a learning-resource path, so "what now?" has an actual answer instead of just a label.

Before you tell us anything

What happens to what you share

You're about to answer 60 fairly personal questions and possibly talk to an AI about your future. Here's exactly what we do with that.

Your password is never stored as text

It's hashed with PBKDF2-SHA256 before it ever touches the database. Nobody on our side can read it.

Encrypted in transit, hosted in East Africa

Every connection runs over HTTPS/TLS 1.3, and your data is stored on servers within the region.

Never sold, never shared with advertisers

No third party gets your assessment results or profile. There's no ad business here to sell it to.

Your AI coach chats stay yours

Conversations are private to your account and are not used to train any AI model. You can request full deletion at any time.

Before you ask

Frequently asked questions

The questions people actually have before creating an account — not after.

An AI-powered career guidance platform built for Kenyan students and anyone exploring a career change. It combines a 60-question, MBTI-based personality assessment with Kenyan labour-market data to produce ranked career matches, then backs that up with an AI career coach, subject guidance, and learning-path resources — all free.

Click "Get started free," enter your name, email, and a password, and choose whether you're a Student (adds optional school and subject fields) or a General User exploring options. It takes about two minutes, and you're logged in immediately afterward.

The assessment, your career matches, the AI coach, and the learning-path resources are all free for registered accounts — no credit card, no premium tier you'll run into later. No hidden catch.

Most people finish in 15–20 minutes across 60 questions. Your answers save after every question, so closing your browser or losing signal mid-way is fine — come back and it resumes exactly where you stopped.

Primarily Kenyan secondary and tertiary students, plus anyone reconsidering their career direction. Registration currently supports Student and General User accounts.

Your password is hashed (never stored as plain text), every connection is encrypted, and your assessment results and coach conversations are not sold, shared with advertisers, or used to train any AI model. You can request complete account deletion at any time. See the Privacy section above for the full detail.
